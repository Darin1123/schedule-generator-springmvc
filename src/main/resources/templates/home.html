What is it?
Schedule Generator, as its name implies, helps users generate schedules with users' courses. Users can post courses so that other users may reuse them instead of rewrite new ones.
How does it work?
First, register an account. Then log in to post your course or search courses posted by other people. You can modify, delete, update your courses, and more importantly, you can generate your own schedules!!! If you post a new course, then you will be the author of this course. If someone else selects the course you post, then this course will get a star, and will be more likely to seen by the others.
